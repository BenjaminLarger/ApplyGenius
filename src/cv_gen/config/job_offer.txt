AI Agent Engineer II
Zendesk - Remote, Germany

About Zendesk:
Zendesk is a service-first CRM company that builds support, sales, and customer engagement software designed to foster better customer relationships. From large enterprises to startups, we believe that powerful, innovative customer experiences should be within reach for every company, no matter the size, industry or ambition.

Job Description:
We are looking for an AI Agent Engineer to join our AI team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and optimizing AI-powered agents that enhance our customer service platform. You will work with state-of-the-art language models, develop sophisticated prompting techniques, and create systems that enable these AI agents to interact with tools, APIs, and other services.

Key Responsibilities:
• Design and implement AI agents using large language models (LLMs) like GPT-4, Claude, and other foundation models
• Develop and optimize prompting strategies to achieve reliable and high-quality outputs
• Create systems that allow AI agents to interact with external tools, APIs, and databases
• Build evaluation frameworks to measure agent performance and continuously improve results
• Collaborate with product, design, and research teams to define agent capabilities and user experiences
• Implement safeguards and controls to ensure responsible AI use
• Stay current with the latest developments in LLMs and AI agent architectures
• Contribute to the development of agent frameworks and infrastructure

• Experience integrating AI systems with external services and APIs
